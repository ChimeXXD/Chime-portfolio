How I Work

  • 1. Strategy

    Ask first. I always ensure that we're asking the right questions and taking on the right challenges before diving into design. This phase also involves consulting, exploring potential solutions, and conducting market research and competitive analyses.

  • 2. Design

    Problem solving. You'll have a pixel-perfect design for your app or website at the conclusion of this phase. I develop prototypes that simulate the final results before development as I move from wireframes to the final design.

  • 3. Quality assurance

    Transparent process. Full breakdown of my design procedure through regular meetings. Following the initial design iterations, I'm happy to conduct user testing with your customers to ensure the best solutions are being provided for their needs.
